# Sample Voice Conversation (Filipino)

This transcript demonstrates a real interaction between the AI-powered **Health Insurance Lead Qualifier** and a prospective customer in **Filipino (Tagalog)**. The conversation showcases multilingual support, knowledge retrieval, objection handling, and human-agent escalation.

---

## Call Summary

**Language:** Filipino (Tagalog)

**Duration:** ~1 minute 40 seconds

**Scenario:** A prospective customer without existing health insurance enquires about available insurance options, expresses concerns about premium costs, and requests to speak with a human agent.

### Conversation Highlights

- Customer confirmed they do not currently have health insurance.
- Assistant introduced available insurance options.
- Customer expressed concern about expensive premiums.
- Assistant acknowledged the concern and consulted the knowledge base.
- Customer asked about more affordable plans.
- Assistant explained that specific pricing information was unavailable and offered a follow-up with a licensed insurance agent.
- Customer requested to speak with a human representative.
- Assistant offered to schedule a callback at the customer's preferred time.

---

## Transcript

### Assistant

Hi po, si Alex ito galing sa HDFC. Tumatawag po ako tungkol sa isang insurance option na maaaring bagay sa inyo. May ilang minuto po ba kayo?

### User

Hi po. Wala pa po akong insurance.

---

### Assistant

Okay.

### User

Gusto ko lang malaman ang options.

---

### Assistant

Salamat po sa interest ninyo. May iba't ibang health insurance options na maaaring iayon sa inyong pangangailangan.

Pagkatapos maghanap sa knowledge base...

May gusto po ba kayong particular na goal? Halimbawa, basic health protection, family coverage, o iba pang insurance benefits?

### User

Mahal masyado yung premium.

---

### Assistant

Naiintindihan ko po.

### User

Meron ba kayong mas mura?

---

### Assistant

Wala po akong specific na pricing details sa ngayon, pero maaari ko pong ipa-follow up kayo sa isang licensed insurance agent.

Maaari rin po ninyong sabihin ang inyong budget o preferred coverage upang matulungan kayong makahanap ng pinakaangkop na plan.

### User

Pwede bang makausap yung taong mismo, hindi bot?

---

### Assistant

Oo po. Maaari ko po kayong i-connect sa isang insurance specialist na makakatulong sa inyo.

### User

Ay teka lang po. Hindi pa puwede ngayon. Mamaya na lang tayong mag-usap.

---

### Assistant

Walang problema po. Maaari ko pong i-schedule ang tawag ng aming licensed agent sa oras na pinaka-convenient para sa inyo. Sabihin lamang po kung anong araw at oras ang gusto ninyo.
